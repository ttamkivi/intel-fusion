# Additional Open-Source Resources

Curated publicly available reference materials for military intelligence processing, NATO standards, and Russian military capabilities. All materials are UNCLASSIFIED.

## SALUTE Reporting Standards

- [The SALUTE Report (PDF)](http://davidbrentthompson.com/Military/ScrapBook-Collections-Miscellaneous/Guidelines%20Instructions%20%20Procedures%20and%20Related/The%20SALUTE%20Report.pdf) — Comprehensive Size, Activity, Location, Unit, Time, Equipment format guide
- [FM 21-75 Ch.6: Combat Intelligence](https://www.globalsecurity.org/military/library/policy/army/fm/21-75/Ch6.htm) — Army field manual on intelligence reporting
- [SALUTE Report Training (CTC Bulletin)](https://www.globalsecurity.org/military/library/report/call/call_96-1_ct3-4art.htm) — Combat Training Center guide
- [iSALUTE (U.S. Army INSCOM)](https://www.usainscom.army.mil/iSALUTE/iSALUTEFORM/) — Interactive SALUTE form tool

- [FM 2-22.3 (Marines.mil)](https://www.marines.mil/Portals/1/Publications/FM%202-22.3%20%20Human%20Intelligence%20Collector%20Operations_1.pdf) — Marine Corps edition

- [NATO APP-6(C) Joint Military Symbology (2011)](https://ia601602.us.archive.org/22/items/23-miscellanea/APP-6(C)%20NATO%20Joint%20Military%20Symbology%20-%20May%202011.pdf) — Updated standard
- [AJP-2.1: Allied Joint Doctrine for Intelligence](https://jadl.act.nato.int/ILIAS/data/testclient/lm_data/lm_152845/Linear/JISR04222102/sharedFiles/AJP21.pdf) — NATO intelligence procedures
